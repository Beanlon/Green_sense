What is Green Sense?

GreenSense is a smart platform that helps small greenhouse farmers track their sales and expenses and turn them into useful insights. It shows which crops are most profitable, where costs are too high, and how to plan better for each season. With simple reports and helpful alerts, GreenSense makes managing your farm easier, reduces waste, and helps you grow more efficiently.
